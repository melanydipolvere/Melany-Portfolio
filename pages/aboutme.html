Hello!

My name is Melany Di Polvere, and I'm a designer originally from Argentina, currently living and working in the country. I have a strong passion for nature, music, reading, and podcasts, which keeps me both informed and entertained.

My focus lies in UX/UI design, where I combine my background in Psychology with specialization in legal tech and legal design. I work as a Legaltech & Design professional at Alfaro Abogados, where I'm also a part of the diversity and inclusion committee.

I love using my creative skills and understanding of human behavior to bring innovative solutions to the table. Whether it's designing user interfaces or working on legal tech projects, I'm dedicated to making a positive impact.

When I'm not working, you can find me enjoying the outdoors, listening to music, diving into a good book, or exploring the latest podcast trends. I'm always on the lookout for new ideas and opportunities to learn and grow.
